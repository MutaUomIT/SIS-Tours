Sigiriya ( Lion Rock ) in Dambulla Sri Lanka

Most people say 8th wonder of the world , a UNESCO World Heritage Sites , The Sigiriya is one of the most visited attraction in Sri Lanka by the tourist . In ancient times this was a Buddhist temple , later it was turned in to a big fortress. Still it’s unknown , how the technology was used to built it and the irrigation system developed to bring the water to the top of the rock . You can see still some of the fountains are working.

Blue Whales & Dolphin watching

Sri Lanka may be the only country you can see the world’s biggest land mammal, the elephant, and its largest marine mammal, the blue whale, in a single day. Head over to Mirissa , down south in Sri Lanka. Just for your note , don’t expect a luxury Yoat for your trip , most of the time you will get a seated fishing boat . Also there is a time of the year you can see the blue whales .Mirissa whale watching season begin from mid of November till April of next year . So better plan your itinerary.

Adam’s Peak

Also know as butterfly mountain , Sri Pada . It is well known for the Sri Pada, i.e., “sacred footprint”, a 1.8 m (5 ft 11 in) rock formation near the summit, which in Buddhist tradition is held to be the footprint of the Buddha, in Hindu tradition that of Shiva and in Islamic and Christian tradition that of Adam, or that of St. Thomas. Source:Wikipedia It’s a 8KM journey , the best experience you will get is when you reach to the top of the mountain , the view you will get is unforgettable . You should climb the mountain in mid night , so you can get to the top by early morning . In that way you will be able to see the sun rise , know to one of the best views of the sun rise in the world. Remember most of the time next day should be a relaxing day for you.

Temple of tooth and Kandy Cultural show

Sri Dalada Maligawa or the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic is a Buddhist temple in the city of Kandy, Sri Lanka. It is located in the royal palace complex of the former Kingdom of Kandy. You will get a picture of the Sri Lankan history and traditions around the temple of tooth.

Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

Get close to Elephants , you have chance to feed Elephant babies and to watch them on their bath time . “Pinnawala is notable for having the largest herd of captive elephants in the world. In 2011, there were 88 elephants, including 37 males and 51 females from 3 generations, living in Pinnawala” Source.
